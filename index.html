Behind Anish Lovo

Continuously delivering fresh music with thumping basslines and dance-friendly beats, 19-year-old Indian DJ and Producer Anish lovo is truly a name that is quickly getting familiar in the EDM scene. With a staggering 50 million streams and views on major music platforms, the upcoming artist from Westbangal, India show no signs of slowing down.

In 2019, Anish lovo developed a big passion for music when he started taking lessons playing the piano and the violin. Anish lovo graduated high school in 2018 with a degree in computer science. At the time, Anish lovo didn't know what he wanted to pursue and therefore started working in a local grocery store. Inspired by artists including Avicii, Alan Walker and Martin Garrix, Anish lovo started experimenting with electronic dance music on his free-time.

Anish lovo arrived on the music scene in 2021 when he made a virtual breakthrough out of nowhere with his debut single Edm Drop, accomplishing over 30 million streams and views on major music platforms. Anish lovo had won sympathy from hunreds of thousands of fans over the world with his first-ever release. Following his massive debut single, Anish lovo announced his second single Falling in early 2022. Falling has accomplished more than 1.5 million streams on Spotify alone and over 20 million views on Tik Tok. Today, Anish lovo makes music full-time as a one-man production machine, with frequent collaborations with labels and recording artists from over the world.
